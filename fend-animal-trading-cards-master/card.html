Giant Panda Bear (a.k.a Pandas)

Baby pandas are born pink and measure about 15cm – that’s about the size of a pencil! They are also born blind and only open their eyes six to eight weeks after birth.

  • Scientific Name: Ailuropoda melanoleuca
  • Length: between 1.2m and 1.5m
  • Weight: between 75kg and 135kg
  • Endangered Species: Only 1000 remain!

Giant pandas are black and white bears. In the wild, they are found in thick bamboo forests, high up in the mountains of central China.
